WHY ELEVATE

The Elevate Program was created to support the Penn State students hungry to transform their daily lives toward greater professional purpose and personal well-being.

WHAT YOU GET

A guaranteed seat at experiential workshops in skills and knowledge new to State College.

Private dinners with the awesome workshop facilitators to deepen what you’ve learned.

A community of peers where you’ll belong, have a blast, and get support as you grow.

One-on-one advising to help you take next steps on doing what matters most to you.

A coworking membership at New Leaf to turn your insights into impact.
