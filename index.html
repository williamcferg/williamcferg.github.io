Who is this program for?

The Elevate Program was created to support the Penn State students hungry to transform their daily lives toward greater professional purpose and personal well-being.

Two types of exceptional individuals currently struggle to match their potential in the university system. One is the student for whom class is too easy, conversations are too plain, and corporate internships are passe. They have the GPA, the advisor, the approval, and want whatâ€™s next/more. The other is the person who needs more creative environments than the classroom.
