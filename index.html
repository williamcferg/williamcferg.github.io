8 WEEKS. 4 WORKSHOPS. INFINITE WISDOM.

Hosted at New Leaf Initiative in partnership with Lion Launch Pad, the Division of Undergraduate Studies, the College of Engineering, and the College of Health and Human Development.

WHY ELEVATE

The Elevate Program was created to support the Penn State students hungry to transform their daily lives toward greater professional purpose and personal well-being.

WHAT YOU GET

Guaranteed seats at experiential workshops in skills and knowledge never before brought to State College.

Private catered dinners with the workshop facilitators to dive deeper into what you’ve learned.

A community of peers where you’ll belong, have a blast, and get support even in your hardest times.

One-on-one advising to help you take next steps on doing what matters most to you.

Coworking membership at New Leaf Initiative to pursue your ideas and meet even more allies.
